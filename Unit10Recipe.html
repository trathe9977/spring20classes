Bacon Wrapped Maple Pork Loin

Ingredient Amount Picture of the goods
Boneless Pork Loin 2 1/2 pounds
Salt 1/2 tsp
Black pepper 1/2 tsp
Bacon 6-8 pieces
Maple Syrup Enough to cover
Salt and pepper the pork loin as evenly as possible. Wrap the bacon slices tightly around the pork, tucking the loose ends underneath. Place in a 225 degree oven (in a baking dish) or smoker for 2 1/2 hours, or until the internal temperature reaches 135 degrees. Bring the heat up to 400 and pour the maple syrup in a nice even coating over the pork loin, cooking until the internal temp reaches 145 degrees and the syrup cooks into a glaze. Remove from the heat and let it reast for 5-10 minutes, then carve it up and enjoy.
