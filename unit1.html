My Favorite Movie Trilogy

By far, my favorite movie trilogy is the original Star Wars saga. I remember seeing Star Wars for the first time at a drive-in theater. Darth Vader was 40 feet tall, and I was hooked. Two sequels followed to complete the original trilogy:

  1. Star Wars: Episode IV - A New Hope
  2. Star Wars: Episode V - The Empire Strikes Back
  3. Star Wars: Episode VI - Return of the Jedi

Over the years I have been in many discussions with friends and family regarding which is better, Star Wars or Star Trek. I am not saying that I don't like Star Trek, or other big ticket trilogies such as The Lord of the Rings. I have always preferred the films of the Star Wars universe for several reasons:

  • Varied and exotic alien species
  • Innovative ship designs
  • Strange new planets
  • A grittier, lived-in feeling to the universe
