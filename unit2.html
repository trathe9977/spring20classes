Battlefields in the United States

There are many locations scattered across the USA that have been the site of armed conflicts. Most people automatically think of the American Revolution or the Civil War, but there are battlefields from many other conflicts as well.

Some of the other wars with battles on American soil

  1. Mexican-American War
    1. Battle of San Gabriel (CA, 1847)
    2. Battle of Taos (NM, 1847)
    3. Battle of Palo Alto (TX, 1846)
  2. War of 1812
    1. Siege of Detroit (MI 1812)
    2. Burning of Washington, D.C. (1814)
    3. Battle of New Orleans (LA, 1815)
  3. Second World War
    1. Bombing of Pearl Harbor (HI, 1941)
    2. Battle of Attu Island (AK, 1943)

Many battlefields have been lost to real estate development. There are organizations that work to preserve these lands for the future, allowing us to visit these locations and see where history was made.
